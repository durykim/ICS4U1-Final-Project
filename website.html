Other Westmount

Westmount Secondary School

Located in Hamilton, Ontario stands a building that serves as a school to 1000+ students in grades 9-12. Here lives countless memories of thousands spanning from 1961 to present day. Although students may say they hate coming here every morning at 8:40am, there is no denying the resilient community that resides here. Whether they like it or not, everyone who comes through here will forever be a Wildcat... 🐯 I personally have enjoyed my time at Westmount, and now that I am graduating soon, I wanted to create this project to commemorate the school I have been attending for the past 4 years.
